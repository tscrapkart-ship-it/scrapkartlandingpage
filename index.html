What ScrapKart is

Scrap is value. Most of India still treats it as waste — landfilled, burned, or quietly bought at undervalued prices. ScrapKart is the missing infrastructure: a digital exchange where lots get listed, verified buyers bid in the open, and settlement clears in 72 hours.

Verified buyers

Every recycler is CPCB and EPR-checked before they get the keys.

72-hour settlement

Bid accepted today, money in the bank by the third working day.

Scale-agnostic

A household drop-off of newsprint or a 30-tonne foundry pickup — same disciplined process.
